So you think your town need a Tough Guy Book Club chapter?
That’s good because that’s how TGBC grows, and so we’ve made it quick and easy.

Step 1
Get in contact with us. Send us an email or a message via Facebook. All of our contact details are on the Contact page.

Step 2
We’ll be in contact with you so we can have a chat about club stuff. We like to have a chat to get to know a bit about you and your town, don’t want ya life story, just want to make sure you’re not a cannibal or a massive dickhead or anything.

Step 3
We’ll send you everything you need to run your Chapter, such as books, flyers, merch, etc. we’ll teach you how to run a club, set you up to get your monthly books and most importantly we’ll help you find goons for your Chapter.

Step 4
Start running that thing.
But remember, from the moment you start your local Chapter you have the full support of the Tough Guy Book Club and we’ve found there are very few problems we can’t fix when we’re all together.
